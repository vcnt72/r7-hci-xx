    Rainbow 7 Quarantine is a five-player tactical co-op game set several years in the future of the Rainbow 7 Universe. Rainbow 7 Quarantine is being developed by an entirely new team at Ubipost to develop something that is truly more than the sum of its parts.

    The Operators will face off against a new breed of mutated alien parasite. Infecting human hosts and their surroundings, this new enemy is more lethal and challenging than ever before. Prepare to launch into tense, chaotic and totally unpredictable missions as you and your squad risk everything.

    Rainbow 7 Quarantine will included in Pre-Order Edition of Rainbow 7 Siege or available for sale as Downloadable Content (DLC) after its release. Available to play at July 10th 2020.
